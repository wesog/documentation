<Warning>This filter is not ready yet. It will be available in future realeases of Wesog Search.</Warning>

Filter and subfilters values:

- `a` (author): Name of the author or `anyone`
  - `g` (gender): `man` or `woman`
  - `n` (nationality): ISO 3166-1 alpha-2 `fr`, `gb`, `` 


## Examples

- `a:Vincent van Gogh` Filter artwotks painted by Vincent van Gogh.
- `a:Steve McCurry` Filter photograhs taken by Steve McCurry.
- `a:anyone[n:nl]` Filter by Dutch photographers/painters.
- `a:anyone[g:woman]` Filter by female photographers/painters.
