Dates are defined by the ISO 8601 format. For example September 27, 2022 at 6 p.m. is represented as `2022-09-27 18:00:00.000`

> ### ISO 8601
>
> The ISO 8601 format provides a unique string represtantion of dates and times:
>
> | ISO 8601 Example            | Description                                         |
> |-----------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------|
> | `2024`                      | **Year only**                                       |
> | `2024-01`                   | **Year-Month**                                      |
> | `2024-01-01`                | **Year-Month-Day**                                  |
> | `2024-01-02 15`             | **Year-Month-Day Hour (Local Time)**                |
> | `2024-01-02 15Z`            | **Year-Month-Day Hour (UTC)**                       |
> | `2024-01-01 10:30`          | **Year-Month-Day Hour:Minute (Local Time)**         |
> | `2024-01-01 10:30Z`         | **Year-Month-Day Hour:Minute (UTC)**                |
> | `2024-01-01 10:30:15`       | **Year-Month-Day Hour:Minute:Second (Local Time)**  |
> | `2024-01-01 10:30:15Z`      | **Year-Month-Day Hour:Minute:Second (UTC)**         |
> | `2024-01-01 10:30:15+05:00` | **Year-Month-Day Hour:Minute:Second (Timezone +5)** |
> | `2024-01-02 15:45:30-07:00` | **Year-Month-Day Hour:Minute:Second (Timezone -7)** |


Dates filter can define excat dates, also ranges using the following operators:

- `>` greater than
- `<` less than
- `>=` greater than or equal to
- `<=` less than or equal to

## Examples

- `d:2020-02-08` Only Febraury 8th of 2020
- `!d:2020-02-08` Any day except Febraury 8th of 2020
- `d:<2020-02-08` Lees than Febraury 8th of 2020
- `d:>2020-02-08` Greater than Febraury 8th of 2020
- `d:<=2020-02-08` Lees than or equal to Febraury 8th of 2020
- `d:>=2020-02-08` Greater than or equal to Febraury 8th of 2020
- `!d:<2020-02-08` Greater than or equal to Febraury 8th of 2020 (filter by the negative complement)
- `d:>=2020-02-08 & d:<=2024-02-08` Inclusive range
- `d:>2020-02-08 & d:<2024-02-08` Exclusive range
